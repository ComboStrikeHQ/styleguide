Colors

Swatches

Most situations would use greyscale (our grey/blue colour swatches, or actual greys), with our orange colour as an accent. When used heavily our brand colour can be overwhelming, so try to reserve it for elements that really need the attention.

The yellow secondary colour is used almost exclusively within UI/web-app environments, where more visual feedback is required.

Resize Images

Definitions

Aspect Ratio

The width:height ratio of the image. Generally we want to keep the aspect ratio so that an image can be enlarged without appearing stretched or squashed.

Crop

Cropping is the removal of the outer parts of an image to improve framing, accentuate subject matter or change aspect ratio.

Resizing Tool

Here is a free online tool you can use to resize images:

resizeimage.net

Formats

JPG
  • Small file-size
  • Good for photos
  • Bad for flat-colours and text
  • No transparency
GIF
  • Small file-size
  • Bad for photos
  • Good for flat-colours and text
  • Supports basic transparency
PNG
  • Large file-size
  • Good for everything
  • Supports advanced transparency
